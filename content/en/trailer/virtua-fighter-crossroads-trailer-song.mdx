<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Battle Systems">

While the **virtua fighter crossroads trailer song** sets the mood, the gameplay mechanics define the experience. RGG Studio is preserving the core "Punch, Kick, Guard" philosophy of the series while introducing layers that modern players expect from a high-budget fighting adventure. The combat is split between traditional 1v1 duels and multi-opponent brawls found within the story mode.

The new **Break & Rush** system is a standout feature. By focusing attacks on specific body regions, players can cause lasting damage that carries over between rounds, forcing opponents to adapt their defensive strategy mid-match.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Triangle">
    Understand the core logic: Strikes beat Throws, Guard beats Strikes, and Throws beat Guard. This fundamental Virtua Fighter loop remains the heart of the game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Flow Guard">
    Apply the new **Flow Guard** mechanic to perform stylish, fluid defensive maneuvers. This system is designed to be more visually expressive and intuitive than classic technical parries.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Stunner States">
    Use the **Stunner** strike to open up the opponent. This leads directly into the **Stun Combo** flow, allowing for high-damage sequences without overly complex inputs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Break & Rush">
    Target specific limbs to accumulate damage. Once a "Break" occurs, the opponent's effectiveness is reduced, providing a strategic advantage for the remainder of the fight.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Tip">
For players transition from other RGG Studio games, the **Uprising Rules** mode offers a more momentum-based experience compared to the strict round-based **Standard Rules**. Use Uprising to experience a more cinematic and dramatic match flow.
</Callout>
