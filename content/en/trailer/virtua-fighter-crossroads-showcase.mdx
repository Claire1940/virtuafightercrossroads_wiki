<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Battle Systems">

The **virtua fighter crossroads showcase** detailed a complete overhaul of the combat engine. While the core philosophy of Punch, Kick, and Guard remains, new systems have been added to facilitate the "Fighting Adventure" gameplay. The most notable addition is the "Break & Rush" system, which introduces persistent damage across rounds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Triangle">
    Understand the core logic: Strikes beat Throws, Guard beats Strikes, and Throws beat Guard. This fundamental balance is the heart of Crossroads.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Break & Rush">
    Target specific body regions to build "Break" damage. Once a part is broken, it stays weakened for the rest of the match, opening new combo routes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Learn Flow Guard">
    Use the new defensive mechanic to parry attacks with cinematic flair. This shifts the momentum and sets up the "Stunner" state for counter-attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Stun Combos">
    Follow up a Stunner with the new intuitive combo system. This allows for high-damage sequences without requiring frame-perfect inputs from legacy titles.
  </Step>
</Steps>
