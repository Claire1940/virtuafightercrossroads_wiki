<Section icon={Map} title="Combat Mechanics and Control Systems">

At the heart of the **virtua fighter crossroads vs tekken 8** debate is the mechanical depth of their control schemes. Virtua Fighter has historically used a deceptively simple three-button layout (Punch, Kick, Guard). Despite fewer buttons, the system demands extreme precision, utilizing frame-perfect buffering and complex directional inputs. Tekken 8 utilizes a four-button layout mapping directly to the character’s limbs, offering highly expressive, long-string juggle combos.
