<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Gameplay Mechanics and Combat Systems">

The core combat systems preserve the series' legendary focus on close-range reads while introducing modern mechanics that emphasize momentum and defense. The classic strike-throw-guard triangle remains the foundation of every exchange. Strikes interrupt throw attempts, guarding stops incoming strikes, and throws punish passive defensive play. Within this framework, several new systems reshape how matches unfold.

The visual presentation has also been overhauled. Action-movie camera angles and dramatic hit reactions make critical moments easier to read without compromising the technical precision that competitive players expect.

<Callout type="warning" title="Persistent Damage">
The broken status inflicted by the Break & Rush system carries over across rounds, meaning early mistakes can severely impact your survival later in the match.
</Callout>

  <Step num="1" title="Master the Combat Triangle">

  <Step num="2" title="Practice Basic Movement">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Tutorial Mode">

  </Step>
