<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: The New Gold Standard">

At its core, **virtua fighter crossroads rated m** remains a precision-based 3D fighter. The gameplay is built on the foundation of *Virtua Fighter 5: Ultimate Showdown* but introduces "new toys" for every character. The animation quality remains best-in-class, with RGG Studio adding cinematic flare to the traditional combat flow.

**Key Gameplay Innovations:**
- **Limb Damage System**: Target specific body parts to reduce an opponent's effectiveness (e.g., damaging legs to slow movement).
- **Reactionary Flow State**: A refined blocking system that reacts to specific punch heights and angles.
- **Environmental Physics**: Real-time destruction during fights that affects positioning and ring-outs.
- **Reduced Air Juggles**: A shift toward standing crumbles and ground interactions for a more "grounded" feel.
