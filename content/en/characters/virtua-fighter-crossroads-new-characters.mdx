<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Systems and Character Mechanics">

The gameplay in *Virtua Fighter Crossroads* preserves the series' legendary close-range spacing and strike-throw-guard triangle while introducing modern mechanics designed to enhance tactical depth. The combat system is split into two distinct rule options: Standard Rules and Uprising Rules. Standard Rules provide the classic three-round, health-depletion, and ring-out experience that veterans expect. Uprising Rules, on the other hand, introduce dynamic momentum-shifting opportunities that reward aggressive play and quick reactions.

To help players transition to these new systems, we have outlined a step-by-step progression path for mastering the core combat mechanics.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Strike-Throw-Guard Triangle">
    Understand the foundational logic: strikes beat throw attempts, guarding nullifies incoming strikes, and throws punish passive guarding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize the Stunner and Stun Combo">
    Practice the new Stunner strike technique. Landing a Stunner opens up a fluid Stun Combo window, allowing you to execute high-damage follow-ups without complex inputs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Break & Rush Pressure">
    Target specific body regions on your opponent. Accumulating damage on a limb triggers a break attack, causing debuffs that carry over across rounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Integrate Flow Guard into Defense">
    Activate Flow Guard to execute stylish, active defensive maneuvers. This defensive tool works alongside Break & Rush to help you shift from defense to offense seamlessly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Break & Rush Caution">
Because body-part damage carries over between rounds, failing to defend against targeted limb strikes can severely handicap your character's mobility or offensive power later in the match.
</Callout>
