<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Mastering the Killer's Style">

The **Virtua Fighter Crossroads Bakunawa Killer** utilizes a combat style that emphasizes the game's new mechanics: **Break & Rush** and **Flow Guard**. While legacy characters rely on established Bajiquan or Jeet Kune Do forms, the killer uses a pragmatic, high-velocity style designed to dismantle opponents quickly.

### The Break & Rush System
This mechanic is central to the killer's gameplay. By targeting specific body regions, the killer can accumulate "part damage." Once a threshold is reached, a **Break Attack** can be triggered, inflicting a debuff that carries over between rounds.

| Mechanic | Category | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stunner** | Strike | A rapid opener that connects into the **Stun Combo** flow. |
| **Flow Guard** | Defense | An active parry that creates openings for immediate counter-attacks. |
| **Uprising Rules** | Battle Rule | A momentum-based scoring system where the killer excels at rapid-fire points. |
| **Stun Combo** | Offensive | A fluid sequence of hits that bypasses traditional juggle physics for realism. |
