<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Mechanics and Battle Systems">

Virtua Fighter Crossroads maintains the series' core philosophy—Punch, Kick, and Guard—while introducing several new layers that emphasize momentum and cinematic flair. The battle system is split into two primary rulesets: **Standard** and **Uprising**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Strike-Throw-Guard Triangle">
    The foundational logic remains: Strikes beat throws, Guards block strikes, and Throws punish passive guarding. This close-range read system is the heart of Virtua Fighter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Break & Rush">
    Target specific body regions to accumulate damage. Once a threshold is met, a **Break Attack** can be landed, causing status effects that persist across rounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Flow Guard">
    A new defensive mechanic that allows for fluid motion and stylish counters. It is designed to work in tandem with the Break & Rush system to shift momentum.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Stunner and Stun Combos">
    Use the **Stunner** technique to open up opponents. This leads into **Stun Combos**, which are more intuitive and visually expressive than traditional juggle routes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The **Uprising Rules** are a significant addition, designed as a dynamic alternative to the traditional round-based format. In this mode, momentum shifts are more frequent, and players are rewarded for aggressive, high-risk playstyles that mirror the action-movie presentation of the story mode.

| Mechanic | Type | Effect | Player Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Break & Rush** | Offensive | Regional body damage | Creates long-term strategy across matches |
| **Flow Guard** | Defensive | Fluid movement/counters | Makes defense more active and readable |
| **Stun Combo** | Combo | High-damage follow-ups | Simplifies execution for newer players |
| **Uprising Rules** | Battle Rule | Momentum-shifting flow | Ideal for dramatic, fast-paced matches |
| **Standard Rules** | Battle Rule | Classic round-based | Best for competitive, technical play |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Advice">
New players should focus on the **Stunner** technique. It provides a clear window for damage without requiring the complex frame-perfect inputs seen in previous Virtua Fighter entries.
</Callout>
