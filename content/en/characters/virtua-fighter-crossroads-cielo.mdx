<Section icon={Shield} title="Beginner Training and Transition Guide">

Transitioning from traditional 2D fighting games or even older 3D titles to Virtua Fighter Crossroads requires a solid understanding of the series' fundamental mechanics. Virtua Fighter is built on a "strike, throw, and guard" triangle, where timing and spacing are far more important than memorizing long, complex button inputs. Cielo's boxing-rooted MMA style is exceptionally well-suited for learning these core concepts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Strike-Throw-Guard Triangle">
    Understand that strikes beat throw attempts, guarding blocks incoming strikes, and throws punish passive guarding. Cielo's fast punches are perfect for interrupting opponents who attempt to throw.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Practice High-Mid-Low Defense">
    Standing guard blocks high and mid attacks but loses to low strikes and throws. Crouching guard blocks low strikes and avoids high attacks but is vulnerable to mid-range overheads. Alternate between them based on the opponent's posture.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize the Break & Rush System">
    Focus your attacks on specific body parts. Cielo's continuous punch combinations allow you to rapidly build damage on an opponent's upper body, leading to a broken state that carries over across rounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Incorporate Flow Guard and Stunners">
    Use active defensive movement (Flow Guard) to create openings, then immediately follow up with a Stunner attack to initiate a fluid, high-damage Stun Combo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To help you structure your practice sessions, utilize the following training drills in the game's Tutorial and Practice modes. Consistent practice will ensure you are ready for both the single-player challenges and competitive online matchmaking.

| Drill Name | Focus Area | Practice Action | Target Goal |
| Triangle Drill | Strike-Throw-Guard | Alternate blocking, striking, and throwing against AI | Successfully counter 10 consecutive AI actions |
| Guard Switch | Standing vs. Crouching | Block a mix of mid and low attacks from the dummy | Correctly block 5 consecutive mixed strings |
| Combo Link | Stunner to Stun Combo | Execute a Stunner input followed by a rapid combination | Complete the full string without dropping the combo |
| Spacing Drill | Close-Range Spacing | Backdash to avoid an attack, then immediately punish | Land 5 clean whiff punishes in a row |

<Callout type="info" title="Practice Tip">
Do not rush into online matches immediately. Spend time in Tutorial Mode and Arcade Mode to build muscle memory for Cielo's basic punch-kick-guard flow before testing your skills against human opponents.
</Callout>
